About Us

Team Astro is an esports organization founded in 2024, currently hosting Matcherino tournaments open to everyone.

Our Journey

Since our inception, Team Astro has been dedicated to growth and excellence. We've celebrated key milestones, most notably, winning our first Matcherino Tournament. As our community continues to grow, we are constantly looking for ways to improve and take on new challenges.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a leading force in Brawl Stars, known for our skill, professionalism, and positive impact on the community. Team Astro is a passionate group dedicated to having fun and competing in tournaments. We strive for excellence while fostering a supportive environment.
